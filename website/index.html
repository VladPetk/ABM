How to read

Each dot is one person. Where they sit shows their views — redistributive vs free-market on the horizontal axis, cultural liberal vs conservative on the vertical. Colour shows which party. Saturation rises with conviction: people at the extremes pop, those near the centre fade.

How to play

Pick a story on the left. Open Parameters (top-left of the sim) to change how the society works — how open-minded people are, how loyal they feel to their party, how strong partisan media is. Hover any dot to meet them; click to pin.
